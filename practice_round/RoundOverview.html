# Expert Client
1 The experts sets the prices by choosing one of three price vectors. The client decides if he or she wants to enter the market.
2 The expert diagnoses all three clients, receives the signal about each client’s problem, and decides if he or she wants to implement the high-quality or the low-quality treatment. The client observes all expert prices, each expert’s ability levels, and decides which expert to approach.
3 Payoffs are realized. Clients are treated according to the treatment decision of their chosen expert. Experts charge each client that approached them.

You will now complete a practice round. This round does not affect your bonus payoff. It is solely meant for you to familiarize yourself with the decision environment.
